The 2019 Road Condition Mix of 32% Good, 49% Fair and 19% Poor suggests the road condition has deteriorated significantly compared to the last four (4) years. The current condition mix lags far behind the projected target of 70% Good, 20% Fair and 10% Poor to be achieved by 2037, as contained in the GHA’s 20-Year Strategic Plan (2017 – 2037). This may be attributed to inadequate budget funding, late procurement and commencement of road maintenance projects, misplaced maintenance intervention selection and prioritization, suspension of projects due to delayed or non-payment of contractors among others as the possible factors.
